Gear Reducer with Straight Teeth

This project focuses on the 3D design and modeling of a gear reducer with straight teet. The objective was to create an optimized mechanical transmission system using SolidWorks, ensuring accurate dimensions and realistic material properties.

The model includes housing, shafts, bearings, and gears, all designed according to engineering standards. The gear reducer is intended for industrial applications, where precise motion control and high torque are required.

Technologies Used

  • SolidWorks
