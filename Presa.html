3D Model - Rotary Nut Press

The Rotary Nut Press is a mechanical system designed for precise fastening applications. It features a rotating nut mechanism, a threaded spindle for controlled movement, and a reinforced steel structure for durability. The press applies up to 12.9 kN of force with a 140 mm stroke length, making it ideal for industrial assembly and mechanical operations. Safety features include lubrication, corrosion protection, and chamfered edges for operator safety.
